Questions to ask

Helpful answers before you book.

Here are the most important things customers ask before choosing a home cleaning service.

verifiedAre you licensed, bonded, and insured?

Yes. MA Cleaners operates professionally and takes customer trust seriously. We recommend every homeowner work with a cleaning company that is properly insured and prepared to protect your home.

securityDo you perform background checks on your cleaners?

We focus on sending reliable, respectful, and trustworthy cleaning professionals. Our team is selected with care so customers feel comfortable inviting us into their homes.

checklistWhat is included in your standard cleaning service?

Standard cleaning usually covers common living areas, bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchens, counters, sinks, floors, dusting, trash removal, and general surface cleaning. We confirm the final checklist before service.

infoWhat is not included in the cleaning service?

Heavy buildup, inside appliances, inside cabinets, deep grout work, high windows, laundry, and special projects may require extra time or a deep cleaning service. We always clarify this before starting.

paymentsHow is pricing calculated, and are there additional fees?

Pricing depends on the home size, condition, number of rooms, service type, and whether it is one-time or recurring. If extra work is needed, we explain it clearly before moving forward.

cleaning_servicesDo you provide all cleaning supplies and equipment?

Yes, we can bring standard cleaning supplies and equipment. If you prefer specific products for your home, children, pets, or surfaces, let us know before the appointment.

starDo you offer a satisfaction guarantee?

Yes. Our goal is to leave you happy with the results. If something does not meet expectations, contact us quickly so we can review the issue and make it right.

support_agentHow do I report an issue if I am not satisfied?

Call us and explain what needs attention. Clear communication helps us respond faster, improve service, and make sure your home gets the care you expected.

event_repeatDo you offer recurring cleaning services?

Yes. We offer recurring options for weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly cleaning. Recurring customers can keep a cleaner home with less planning and more consistent results.

reviewsCan you provide customer reviews or references?

Yes. We are happy to point customers to available reviews and feedback. A cleaning service should be able to show real proof of reliability and customer satisfaction.
